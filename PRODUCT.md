# Silver Night Operator — Product Context

## Product Purpose

Silver Night Operator is a weekly cyberpunk synthwave music channel. Each Friday a 1-hour mix publishes on YouTube telling the story of a different Night Operator: a woman protagonist with her own identity, faction, and world in a cyberpunk megacity. The website is the public portal where operator profiles unlock as their YouTube videos go live.

The site is the universe's canonical record — part dossier archive, part faction map, part music discovery portal. Design IS the product here.

## Register

brand

## Users

Cyberpunk and synthwave music fans who discover the channel through YouTube. They arrive curious about the world-building. They browse the operator grid, filter by faction, and dive into individual dossiers to read the lore and listen to the music. The experience should feel like accessing a classified intelligence archive from a cyberpunk megacity — mysterious, immersive, slightly dangerous.

## Brand

**Name:** Silver Night Operator (SNO)

**Tone:** Classified intelligence file meets underground music scene. Cold, precise, atmospheric. Never corporate, never cute.

**Visual identity:**
- Dark background: `#09090e` (near-black with a blue tint)
- Cyan accent: `#00c4ff` — the primary active/unlocked color
- Gold: `#c9a84c` — used in some dossiers for faction flavor
- Faction colors: cyan (Architecture), purple (Ritual Network), green (South Bloc), orange (Iron North), blue-grey (Ghost Layer), red (Forbidden Zone), teal (Velocity), gold (Unaligned)
- Typography: Bebas Neue (display), Inter (body), monospace for metadata/codes
- Aesthetic: classified government archive, CRT screens, neon noir, signal interference

## Anti-references

- No corporate SaaS look
- No gradient text
- No glassmorphism decoratively
- No bouncy animations
- No bright white backgrounds
- No generic music app look (Spotify, Apple Music aesthetic)
- No hero-metric dashboards

## Strategic principles

1. Every unlock is an event — cards reveal themselves as operators go live
2. The dossier is the deeper world — the grid teases, the dossier delivers
3. Faction identity matters — color coding and filtering are world-building tools
4. Classified aesthetic is non-negotiable — this is a secret archive, not a streaming platform
